CUSTOM CLEARANCE AND FORWARDING OF CARGO

All goods that are imported or exported into Southern Africa and other countries require customs clearance.

Customs clearance is a process where by the correct documentation is required to clear your cargo.

We handle all clearing procedures for you including, but not limited to:

  • Shipping line charges
  • Customs entry
  • Port payment and duties
  • Tariffs interpretation
  • Drawback duties

We have a team of experts that process customs clearance ensuring all legal matters are complied with. This is one of the added advantages of utilizing Zarilogitistics, as we offer a variety of logisitcs services and handle other technicalities as well.
